The Provintial Kingdoms

Gujarat

  • Disintegrated from Delhi in 1397 under Zaffar Khan who took the title of Sultan Muzaffar Shah
  • Ahmed Shah I built a new city Ahmedabad and also built Jama Masjid and Tin Darwaza at Ahmedabad
  • Mahmud Beghra was another prominent ruler, under whose rule the Portuguese set up a factory
  • Sanskrit scholar Udayraja was his court poet

Malwa

  • Annexed by Alauddin Khilji in AD 1305
  • Dilawar Khan Ghuri declared independence in 1435
  • Hasan Shah built the Jama Masjid, Hindol Mahal and Jahaz Mahal at Mandu
  • Annexed to the Mughal state in 1562

Kashmir

  • Zainalubedin was the greatest ruler of Kashmir, known as Badshah and the Akbar Of Kashmir
  • Introduced art of shawl making, constructed Zaina lake and artificial island in the wular lake
  • Later ruled by Chak dynasty who submitted to Akbar in 1562

Mewar

  • Capital city of Chittor was captured by Alauddin Khilji in 1303 but Rajput rule was restored by Rana Hamir in 1326
  • Rana Kumbh Karn was the greatest ruler of Mewar
  • He built the famous Vijay Stambh in Chittor to commemorate his vistory over Mahmud Khilji of Malwa
  • Mandan was his court poet
  • He was defeated by Babur in the battle of Khanwa (1527)

Bengal

  • Dissintigrated out of the sultanate from the time of the Mohammed Bin Tughlaq
  • In 1432 Ilias Khan founded the Ilias Sahi Dynasty
