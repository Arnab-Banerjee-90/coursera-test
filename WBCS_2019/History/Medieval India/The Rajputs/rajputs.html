The Rajputs

Origins

  • Emeerged as a powerful force in northers India during the 9th and 10th century
  • Four of the Rajput clans claim to have descended from a mythical figure out of a sacrificial pit in Mt.Abu
    • Chauhans/Chahaman of East Rajasthan
    • Pratihars/Parihars of South Rajasthan
    • Chalukyas/Solankis of Kathiawar
    • Parmars/Pawars of Malwa
  • Most accepted theory is that they were of a foriegn origin, who came as conquerors and settled in west India

Some Important Rajput Rulers

Jai Chand Gadhawal (1169-94)

  • Assisted Muhammad Ghori against Prithwiraj Chauhan in the second battle of Tarrain (1192)
  • Himself killed by Muhammad Ghori in the battle of Chandawar (1194)

Prithwiraj Chauhan (1178-92)

  • Chahamana ruler of Delhi and Agra
  • Fought two battles against Muhammad Ghori
    • First battle of Tarrian (1191) Prithwiraj defeated Muhammad Ghori
    • Second battle of Tarrian (1192) Prithwiraj was defeated and killed by Muhammad Ghori

Bhola Parmar (1010-55) of Malwa

  • Great conqueror and patron of literature
  • Also known as Kaviraj

Architectural works

  • Kandaria Mahadeva temple Khajuraho built by Chandelas of Bundelkhand in 1000
  • Dilwara Temple of Mt.Abu built by Siddharaja Solanki of Gujarat
  • Angkor Vat Temple built by Suryavarman II
  • Sun Temple
