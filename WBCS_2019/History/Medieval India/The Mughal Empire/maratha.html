The Maratha Empire

Shivaji (1627-80)

Conquests
  • Belonged to the Bhonsle clan of Marathas, Father Shahaji Bhonsle and mother Jijabai
  • Trained by Dadaji Kondev and Guru Ramdas
  • Conquered Torna fort in 1646, and built forts at Raigarh, Pratapgarh
  • Killed Afzal Khan in 1659, ambassador of Ali Adil Shah sultan of Bijapur
  • Shaista Khan, deputed by Aurangzeb to curb Shivaji's influence in 1660
  • Shivaji suffered many defeats and lost Poona , till he made a bold attack at night on Shaista's military camp and plundered Surat and later Ahmednagar
  • Raja Jai Singh of Amber was then appointed by Aurangzeb to put down Shivaji (1665), Jai Singh succeeded in besieging Shivaji in Purandhar fort.
  • Treaty of Purandhar was signed in 1665, according to which Shivaji ceded some forts to Mughals, and agreed to help Mughals in their attack on Bijapur
  • Shivaji visited Agra with his son Shambhaji in 1666, was put on house arrest but managed to escape
  • He very soon conquered the forts he had surrendered to the mughals, defeated the mughlas on the Battle of Salher in 1672
  • Coronated on 1674 at Raigarh, took the title of Haindava Dharmodharak and Chatrapati
  • Died in 1680
