Indus Valley Civilization

Nomenclature and Time period

  • Harappan Civilization named by John Marshall
  • Bronze Age civilization
  • 2500 - 1700 B.C

Origins

  • Mehergarh in Baluchistan (Oldest Neolithic settlement in Indian subcontinent 7000 B.C)

Geographical Spread

  • West:-Sutkagendor (Pak-Iran border)
  • East:-Alamgirpur (Uttarpradesh)
  • North:-Manda (Jammu Kashmir)
  • South:-Daimabad (Maharashtra)
  • Mundigak and Shortughai are two sites located in Afganisthan

Script

  • Pictographic
  • Fish symbol mostly represented
  • Alternate Right to left then left to right: Boustrophedon

Agriculture

  • Wheat, barley,dates,peas etc
  • Rice husks found in Lothal and Rangpur (Gujarat)
  • First to produce cotton, called as Sindon by the Greeks

Trade

  • Barter system
  • Linear system of measurement was used
  • Weights and measures were in multiples of sixteen

  • Wheels of toy cart

Chanhudaro (Lancashirte of Indus Civilization) Indus Nawabshah,Sindh,Pakistan Mackey(1925),N.G Mazumdar(1931)

  • Ikkas of bronze

Burial Practices

  • Harappa :- East West axis; R-37 and H cemetary
  • Mahenjodaro :- three forms of burial: Complete, Fractional and post cremation
  • Kalibangan :- Circular and rectangular grave
  • Lothal :- Double burial

Art and Craft

  • Bead making
  • Pottery both plain (red) and painted (red and black)
  • Seals, mostly steatite imagesof humpless bulls,buffalo,tiger
  • Terracota Figurines
