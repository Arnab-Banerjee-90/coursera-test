Foreign Invasion 1

Iranian invasion

Cyrus of Persia (588-530 B.C)

  • Destroyed Capisa (North of Kabul)

Darius-I(516 B.C)

  • Grandson of Cyrus
  • Annexed Punjab,West of Indus and Sind

Xerxes

  • Successor of Darius

Impact of Iranian invasion

  • Cultural exchange in the form of Kharosthi script from Iran to India
  • Impetus to Indo-Iranian trade

Alexander's invasion

  • conquered Kabul in 328 B.C
  • Moved to India via Khyber pass,Reached Orhind in 326 B.C
  • Ambhu,ruler of Taxila readily submitted
  • Met great resistance from Porus in battle of Hydaspas on the banks of Vitasta (Jhelum)
  • After battle of Sakala,proceeded upto Beas,fatigued army refused to cross the river
  • Placed north-westen India under Greek governor Selucus Nikator
  • Remained in India for nineteen months (326-325 B.C)

Impact of Alexander's invasion

  • Indians learnt from Greeks in the field of Astronomy,coinage,architecture and sculpture (Gandhara school)
  • Greek settlements like Alexandria in Kabul and Boukephala in Jhelum
  • Paved the way for Mauryan empire in that area
  • His historians have left valuable accounts related to Indian geography and socio-econimic conditions
