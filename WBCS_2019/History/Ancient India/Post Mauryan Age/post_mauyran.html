Native States

Shunga Dynasty (185 - 73 B.C)

  • Pushyamitra Shunga ruled from Vidisha,defeated Bactrian king Dematrius
  • Known as prosecutor of Buddhism
  • Greek ambassador Heliodorus visited court of fifth Shunga king Bhagabhadra
  • Shunga age marked the revival of the Hindu culture and Sanskrit language,Sanskrit grammarian patanjali was patronised
  • Manusmriti compiled diring this period

Kanva Dynasty (73 - 28 B.C)

  • Minor dynasty founded by Vasudeva, killed last Shunga king Debavuti
  • All rulers were Brahmin
  • Last ruler Susarman, killed by Andhra(satvahana) king Simuka
